04What stood out

↑
Search brand campaigns are the efficiency leaders. "Brand FWD" and "Brand YouGov" delivered conversions at RM 7.16 and RM 11.04 — well under the RM 8.78 account average.
↑
Demand Gen primary-location nearly tripled ROAS (+209% vs May) while cutting spend 86% — a strong efficiency signal, though volume is now small.
!
Three Video campaigns spent RM 3,500+ with zero conversions (brand-primary, brand-secondary, brand-rmkt-primary) — worth confirming whether these are intentional reach-only buys.
!
Search conversions carry no assigned value — 2,235 conversions at RM 0 tracked value understates the account's true blended ROAS.
